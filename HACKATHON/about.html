About Alumni Forum

Welcome to the official Alumni Forum of the National Institute of Technology Sikkim! Our community is a vibrant network of past and present students, dedicated to fostering lifelong connections, professional development, and the spirit of giving back to our alma mater.

Our Mission

The NIT Sikkim Alumni Forum aims to unite all former students under a single platform, enabling them to stay connected with each other and the institute. We strive to facilitate networking opportunities, support ongoing education, and contribute to the overall growth and success of NIT Sikkim.
